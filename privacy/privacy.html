Scope UniPost mobile app
Effective 2026-04-18
Controller UniPost maintainer
Entry page API notes Contact
1

Information We Collect

  • Pinterest account information you authorize, such as username, account ID, board data, and access tokens.
  • Images, tags, upload queues, and posting history you choose to process with the app.
  • Local app preferences such as language, theme, upload fingerprints, and account cache.
2

How We Use the Information

  • To complete Pinterest sign-in, account management, board loading, and image publishing.
  • To identify previously uploaded content locally and reduce duplicate publishing.
  • To preserve your UI preferences and improve app experience.
3

Storage and Transmission

  • Most account and task data are stored locally on your device and are not uploaded to a UniPost-owned server.
  • Pinterest authorization and publishing requests are sent directly to Pinterest after you grant consent.
  • OAuth redirect pages are used only as a callback bridge and are not used for advertising or profiling.
4

Third-Party Services

  • Pinterest — used for account authorization, board retrieval, and pin publishing.
  • GitHub Pages — used to host help pages, privacy policy pages, and release notes.
5

Your Choices and Rights

  • You can remove an account and delete its local data at any time inside the app.
  • You can reset the uploaded-fingerprint index in Settings → Data management.
  • For deletion, correction, or privacy-related questions, contact us through GitHub Issues.
6

Data Retention

Account cache, upload fingerprints, and history are kept locally until you manually remove the account, clear history, or reset the index.

7

Children's Privacy

UniPost is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8

Policy Updates

If our data handling practices change, we will update this page and may notify you in the app or release notes.
