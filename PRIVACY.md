# Privacy Policy for UniPost Studio

**Last updated:** March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how UniPost Studio ("the App", "we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and handles your information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information Collection and Use
UniPost Studio is built as a privacy-first, client-side productivity tool. **We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data, images, or usage analytics on any external servers.** All data generated by the App—including your connected account tokens, posting history, and app preferences—are stored securely and locally on your own device.

## 2. Third-Party Services (Pinterest API)
To provide its core functionality, UniPost Studio connects directly to the official Pinterest API via the secure OAuth 2.0 protocol. 
- The App only requests the permissions strictly necessary to fetch your boards and publish your selected images.
- We do not intercept, view, or store your Pinterest password.
- Your interactions with the Pinterest service through our App are subject to the official [Pinterest Privacy Policy](https://policy.pinterest.com/en/privacy-policy).

## 3. Image Processing
Any images you select within the App for batch uploading or carousel creation are processed entirely locally on your device's memory before being transmitted directly to Pinterest's secure API endpoints. We do not upload your media to any middleman servers.

## 4. Data Security
Because your data is stored locally, the security of your information relies on the security of your device. We highly recommend securing your mobile device with a screen lock, passcode, or biometric authentication.

## 5. Contact Us
If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via our official repository: [https://github.com/joopererer/uni_post](https://github.com/joopererer/uni_post)
